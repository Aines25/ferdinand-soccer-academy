Our Story

Building Champions Since 2010

Ferdinand Soccer Academy was founded with a singular vision: to create an environment where young athletes can develop not just their soccer skills, but their character, discipline, and passion for the beautiful game.

Our holistic approach combines world-class technical training with mental conditioning, ensuring our players are prepared for success on and off the pitch.

What We Offer

Training Programs

From beginners to elite prospects, we have a program designed to elevate every player's game.

Development Academy

Ages 5–13

Weekly coaching sessions run by experienced, FA-qualified coaches. First and foremost, these sessions are great FUN! Perfect for boys and girls already playing for local clubs or those who've never played before.

Learn More →

Advanced Academy

Above Average Players

Professional coaching in 8-week blocks with written assessments. Opportunity to play showcase games against professional academies including Millwall FC, Charlton Athletic, and Gillingham FC.

Learn More →

Goalkeeper Sessions

Specialist Training

Expert goalkeeper coaching to improve the individual keeper. Sessions run alongside outfield training, allowing goalkeepers to also participate in group team play sessions.

Learn More →

1-2-1 & Small Groups

Ages 4–16 • Any Venue

Tailored personal coaching at your local fields or back garden. Improve technique, flexibility, speed, agility & power including dribbling skills, passing, spatial awareness, and fitness.

Book a Session →

Holiday Camps

Under 8s – Under 13s

Football camps during school holidays open to all abilities. Players experience different coaching topics from our professional qualified staff. Outstanding players may be invited to join our 10-week courses.

View Schedule →
